01 — The Gap

TikTok One brought everything under one roof. But the rooms don't connect.

Creators make content. Brands want that content as ads. The path between those two things has too many steps, too many dead ends, and not enough upside for the people doing the creating.

02 — Five Fixes

Product ideas that bridge the gap

Each builds on what TikTok One already has. No new platform. Just smarter connections.

Discovery

Smart Brief Generator

When a brand picks a creator, the campaign brief auto-fills with that creator's top formats, audience data, and what actually works for them. No more blank templates. Cuts brief-to-content from 28 days to under 14.

Monetization

Creator Revenue Bridge

10K-100K creators drive TikTok's most authentic content but earn ~$12/week from Rewards. This creates automated micro-deals: advertisers set a budget and niche, TikTok matches them with mid-tier creators. Guaranteed payouts. Programmatic meets creator economy.

Distribution

One-Click Spark Licensing

When organic content hits a performance threshold, prompt both creator and advertisers: "This is working. License it?" Creator gets paid per impression. Advertiser gets proven creative. TikTok takes a cut. Three winners.

Scale

Localized Creative Adaptation

One creator video becomes five regional versions using TikTok One's existing AI dubbing. Instead of producing per market, adapt what's already working. Up to 60% reduction in creative cost.

Analytics

Performance-Based Creator Score

Replace follower count as the matching signal. Score based on conversion rate, completion rate, audience quality, and brand safety. Mid-tier creators with high performance get the visibility they deserve.

04 — The Landscape

Who's competing for creators?

Hover to compare.

TikTok One

Strong but Fragmented

Best organic reach. Creator-brand matching exists but mid-tier monetization is weak. Content Suite is promising, licensing needs work.

YouTube Shorts

Gaining Fast

Partner Program gives creators a real income floor. Aggressively courting mid-tier creators who can't monetize on TikTok.

Instagram Reels

Simplifying

Creator Marketplace getting easier. Brand deals flow more naturally. Higher per-post rates for creators, weaker discovery.

The Opportunity

TikTok's to Lose

TikTok has the content, the audience, and the ad engine. It just needs to close the loop so everyone gets paid.
